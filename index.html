Amber Jiayu Su

Amber is a undergraduate Architecture student at Cornell University, minoring in Computer Science.

At Cornell, Amber works as a researcher with the Environmental Systems Lab, developing scalable modeling and simulation workflows that address the complexities of the urban built environment. Her research focuses on creating computational and data-driven methods to systematically analyze urban building stock and the synergies among buildings, as well as their impact on occupants; to help cities better adapt to the increasing demands posed by climate change and evolving environmental conditions.
